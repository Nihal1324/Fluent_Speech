About Fluent Speech

Fluent Speech is an affordable, one-step solution designed to help individuals overcome challenges such as stuttering, stage fear, hesitation, and nervousness caused by medical or mental pressure. Our platform combines professional coaching, therapeutic tools, community support, and smart technology to empower you to communicate confidently and clearly.

Our Goal

  • Support individuals who stutter
  • Help overcome stage fear
  • Assist those who fumble or hesitate while speaking
  • Provide aid for nervousness due to medical or mental pressure

Key Features

Speech & Communication Enhancement

  • Professional Coaching: Sessions led by experienced communication coaches.
  • Error Explanations: Detailed analysis of speech errors and guidance to correct them.
  • Communication Flow Checks: Using Gemini API to analyze fluency, tone, and pauses.

Therapeutic & Practice Tools

  • Breathing Exercises: Exercises recommended by medical professionals to reduce anxiety and increase vocal control.
  • Articulation Exercises: Targeted routines to improve clarity of speech.
  • Tongue Twisters: Daily challenges to improve pronunciation and fluency.
  • Mirror Talk Mode: Practice speaking while watching oneself, building confidence and correcting posture/gesture.
  • Role-Play Scenarios: Interactive simulations (e.g., interviews, presentations, group talks).

Health & Supportive Care

  • One-on-One Doctor Consultations: Direct access to speech therapists or psychologists for individual needs.

Community & Motivation

  • Regular Meets: Community video calls or local meet-ups to practice and stay motivated.
  • Community Practice Rooms: Virtual rooms to practice with peers in real-time (voice chat or video).
  • Speech Challenge Tournaments: Gamified competitions to encourage progress and consistency.

Smart Tech Integration

  • AI Chatbot Smart Assistant: A personal assistant that provides tips, reminders, feedback on practice, and answers to user questions.
  • NLP Integration: To analyze user speech and offer personalized training suggestions.
