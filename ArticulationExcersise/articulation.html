Articulation Exercises

How to Practice

To improve your speech clarity and fluency, practice the following articulation exercises daily. Focus on precise mouth movements and clear pronunciation. Repeat each exercise slowly at first, then increase your speed while maintaining accuracy.

Exercises

Smile Stretch: Smile widely, hold for 5 seconds, then relax. Repeat 10 times.

Tongue Out: Stick your tongue out as far as possible, hold for 5 seconds, then relax. Repeat 10 times.

Tongue Side-to-Side: Move your tongue from left to right inside your mouth slowly. Repeat 15 times.

Lip Pucker: Pucker your lips as if to whistle, hold for 5 seconds, then relax. Repeat 10 times.

Cheek Blow: Puff out your cheeks and blow air slowly for 5 seconds. Repeat 10 times.

Tongue Up: Press your tongue to the roof of your mouth and hold for 5 seconds. Repeat 10 times.
